# Structural Cause Indicators v1

WP05.7 converts completed atlas-bound Structural Intelligence evidence into deterministic structural indicators and bounded investigation candidates.

## Purpose

The module answers:

```text
What structural pattern was recorded?
Which controlled investigation domains should be considered next?
```

It does **not** answer:

```text
What is the confirmed physical root cause?
```

## Inputs

```text
Structural Intelligence v1
Spectral Window Structure v1
Structural Drift evidence
```

All reference-bearing inputs must remain bound to the same existing ARBE identity:

```text
Hxxx_Lxxx_Cxxx
```

## Structural indicators

The deterministic rules can record:

- shortwave or longwave balance shift;
- spectral broadening or narrowing;
- shortwave or longwave asymmetric migration;
- localized reflectance elevation, depression or mixed structure;
- broadband reflectance shift;
- oscillatory spectral structure;
- supplied multi-illuminant instability;
- no structural cause indicator under a fully stable assessment.

Each indicator retains its source domain, source status and original evidence explanation.

## Investigation candidates

Indicators can open one or more controlled investigation domains:

```text
PIGMENT_OR_FORMULATION
SUBSTRATE_OR_COATING
PROCESS_CONDITION
MEASUREMENT_SYSTEM
ILLUMINANT_METAMERISM
```

Every candidate is marked:

```text
INVESTIGATION_CANDIDATE_NOT_ROOT_CAUSE
```

Candidate domains are not probability rankings and do not identify a specific pigment, substrate, coating, process fault or measurement fault.

## Deliberate boundary

A single spectral pattern can be compatible with multiple physical causes. Confirmation requires controlled comparison measurements, documented materials, process records and defined measurement conditions.

The result does not establish visual identity, spectral equivalence, recipe approval, certification or production release.
