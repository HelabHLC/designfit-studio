# ARBE λ* Claim Policy

## Identity

Only references matching `Hxxx_Lxxx_Cxxx` are canonical ARBE colour identities. HEX, RGB, Lab, names, brand colours, images and descriptions are requests or communication values.

## Candidate and lock

A candidate is not a reference lock. A recipe is valid only when the applicable AtlasFit evidence confirms the target reference under the declared acceptance gates.

## Prohibited claims

Do not claim:

- generated or AI-designed colour identity;
- Pantone certification or exact Pantone equivalence;
- production approval;
- visual identity or universal material feasibility;
- ownership or redistribution rights for third-party spectral data without explicit evidence.

## Required reporting

Reports must separate the user request, bound reference, candidate, curve evidence, scissor status, metamerism status and final verdict. Missing evidence requires a non-final status.
